## Must Haves Before Entering The Backrooms
----------

- First Aid Kit
- Weapon
- Timer
- Canteen or water bottle
- Snacks
- Sleeping bag
- Durable clothing
- Spare clothes
- Backpack
- Headlamp or flashlight
- Leather work glove
- **Specialized Equipment:**
    - Balaclava or ski mask
    - Thick coat
    - Knee and elbow pads
    - Night vision goggles with light blocker
